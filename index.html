Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we receive from our clients.

What services does DotShot Digital offer?

We offer a full suite of digital services including Web Development, Social Media Marketing, Graphic Design, Video Editing, and Strategic Branding.

How long does a typical website project take?

A standard business website usually takes 2-4 weeks from planning to launch, depending on the complexity and features required.

Do you work with small businesses?

Yes! We love helping local businesses in Indore and beyond grow their digital footprint with affordable, high-impact strategies.

How do you measure the success of a digital marketing campaign?

We track key performance indicators (KPIs) such as conversion rates, click-through rates (CTR), return on ad spend (ROAS), and overall engagement to ensure your investment drives real business growth.

Will my website be mobile-friendly and SEO-optimized?

Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive (works on all devices) and follows modern SEO best practices to help you rank higher on search engines from day one.

Do I get the source files for the graphic design projects?

Yes, upon project completion and final payment, we provide all high-resolution export files (PNG, JPG, PDF) and the original source files (AI, PSD, or Figma) for your records.

Which social media platforms do you manage?

We specialize in Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter. We handle everything from content strategy and post scheduling to community management and paid advertising.

What is included in a Product & Brand Photoshoot session?

Our sessions include professional lighting setup, creative direction, high-end retouching, and delivery of both web-ready and print-ready digital images.

What is the typical turnaround time for video editing?

For short-form content like Reels or Ads, we usually deliver within 48-72 hours. Larger projects like corporate videos or documentaries typically take 1-2 weeks depending on the complexity.
