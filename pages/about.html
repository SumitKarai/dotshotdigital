Meet Our Founder

Shraddha Yadav

Shraddha Yadav is the visionary Owner and Co-Founder of Dotshot Digital, a premier creative and digital marketing agency. With over five years of industry expertise, she has spearheaded Dotshot Digital since 2023, transforming it into a powerhouse for brands seeking a commanding online presence.

A seasoned social media marketer and graphic designer, Shraddha seamlessly blends artistic creativity with data-driven strategy. Her hands-on approach extends to professional photography, where she personally directs and captures visuals to ensure every frame resonates with a brand’s core identity.

Under her inspired leadership, Dotshot Digital has cultivated a high-performance team of specialists in digital marketing, web development, SEO, and cinematic video editing. Her unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation continues to define the agency’s success and the growth of the brands they serve.
